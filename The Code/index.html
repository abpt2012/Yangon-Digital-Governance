Bringing Government Services Closer to You

Access essential services, submit applications, and manage your documents through a secure and centralized platform.

A Smarter Way of Accessing Government Services

Yangon Digital Governance provides a centralized platform for accessing essential government services online. From identity documents to application tracking, our system is designed to simplify processes, reduce waiting times, and improve accessibility for all users.
