Privacy Policy

Welcome to Yangon Digital Governance (YDG). Your privacy and the protection of your personal information are important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and safeguard the information submitted through our platform and digital services. By accessing or using this platform, you acknowledge that you have read, understood, and agreed to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

Information We Collect

YDG may collect personal information required for the delivery of digital government-related services. This information may include names, dates of birth, contact details, addresses, identification numbers, uploaded documents, and account credentials provided by users during registration, application submissions, or account management activities.

In addition to personal information, certain technical information such as login activity, browser type, device information, and system usage data may also be collected to improve platform performance, maintain security, and enhance user experience.

Use of Information

Information collected through the platform is used solely for purposes related to service delivery, account management, application processing, verification, and communication with users. User information may also be used to improve platform functionality, monitor system performance, and ensure the security and reliability of digital services.

YDG does not sell, trade, or misuse personal information submitted through the platform. Information will only be accessed by authorized personnel or systems when necessary for operational, administrative, or security-related purposes.

Data Protection & Security

YDG is committed to protecting user information through appropriate technical and organizational security measures. Personal data and uploaded documents are securely stored using encryption technologies, secure servers, and controlled access systems designed to reduce the risk of unauthorized access, disclosure, or misuse.

While reasonable efforts are made to maintain the security of all information stored on the platform, no online system can guarantee absolute security at all times. Users are encouraged to protect their account credentials and avoid sharing sensitive login information with others.

Confidentiality of Information

All personal information submitted through the platform will be treated with confidentiality and handled responsibly. Access to sensitive user data is limited to authorized individuals and systems directly involved in service processing, technical support, or platform administration.

YDG will take reasonable measures to prevent unauthorized disclosure, modification, or misuse of confidential information. Any sharing of information, where required, will only occur in accordance with applicable procedures, operational requirements, or legal obligations.

Data Retention

User information and submitted documents may be retained for a period necessary to support service delivery, record management, verification processes, and platform operations. Retained data will continue to be protected through appropriate security measures during the storage period.

YDG reserves the right to remove, archive, or manage stored information in accordance with operational, technical, or administrative requirements while maintaining reasonable safeguards for user data.

Policy Updates

YDG may update or revise this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect changes in platform functionality, security practices, or service requirements. The latest version of the Privacy Policy will always be made available through the website, and continued use of the platform after updates indicates acceptance of the revised policy.

If you have any questions or concerns regarding this Privacy Policy or the handling of your information, please contact Yangon Digital Governance at ydgmm@myanmar.gov.mm.
