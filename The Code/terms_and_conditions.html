Terms and Conditions

Welcome to Yangon Digital Governance (YDG). Before using this platform and its services, please carefully review the following Terms and Conditions, as they outline the rules, responsibilities, and conditions governing the use of our website and digital services. By accessing or using this platform, you acknowledge that you have read, understood, and agreed to comply with these Terms and Conditions in their entirety.

Scope of Services

Yangon Digital Governance (YDG) provides a centralized digital platform designed to improve access to selected public and government-related services. Through the platform, users may access services such as document applications, renewals, account management, application tracking, and informational resources related to official documentation and identification services.

The services and features available on the platform may be updated, modified, expanded, or discontinued as necessary to improve functionality and user experience. YDG aims to provide users with a more efficient, accessible, and organized way of managing official services through a secure online environment.

User Responsibilities

Users are responsible for ensuring that all information submitted through the platform is accurate, complete, and truthful. Submission of false, misleading, or incomplete information may result in delays, rejection of applications, or restricted access to services. Users are also expected to use the platform in a lawful and respectful manner.

Users are responsible for maintaining the confidentiality of their login credentials and account information. Any activity conducted through a user account will be considered the responsibility of the account holder. Unauthorized use of the platform, including attempts to disrupt services or gain unauthorized access, is strictly prohibited.

Privacy & Confidentiality

YDG is committed to protecting the privacy and confidentiality of all users. Personal information and uploaded documents submitted through the platform are securely stored and handled using appropriate security measures, including encryption technologies, secure servers, and controlled access systems designed to protect user data.

Information collected through the platform will only be used for purposes related to service delivery, account management, and application processing. YDG will take reasonable measures to safeguard user information and prevent unauthorized access, disclosure, or misuse of personal data.
